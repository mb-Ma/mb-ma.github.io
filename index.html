Short Biography

Minbo Ma is currently reading Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Southwest Jiaotong University. He received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from Southwest Jiaotong University in 2020, the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Chongqing Three Gorges College in 2017.

His research interests lie in data mining, big data analysis with specific focus on urban computing, spatial-temporal data, graph learning, and information extraction.
